Common Questions

My AC is running but not cooling. What's wrong?

Could be low refrigerant, a dirty filter restricting airflow, a frozen coil, or a failing compressor. We'll diagnose it and give you options before doing any work.

How often should I service my AC?

Once a year, ideally in spring before you need it. Regular maintenance catches small problems and keeps your system running efficiently.

Can you add AC to a house that only has a furnace?

Usually, yes. If you have ductwork for your furnace, we can typically add an AC unit that shares those ducts. We'll assess your setup and let you know what's involved.

What size AC do I need?

It depends on your home's square footage, insulation, windows, and layout. We do proper calculations—oversized units cycle too frequently and undersized ones can't keep up.
