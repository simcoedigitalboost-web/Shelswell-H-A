Signs Your AC Needs Attention

Don't wait until your AC fails completely on the hottest day of summer. Watch for these warning signs:

  • Weak airflow or warm air from vents
  • Unusual noises like grinding or squealing
  • Frequent cycling on and off
  • Higher than normal energy bills
  • Excess humidity in your home
  • AC unit is 10+ years old

If you notice any of these issues, give us a call. We'll diagnose the problem and give you honest options.
