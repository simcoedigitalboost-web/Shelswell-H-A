Common Questions

Can you add a gas fireplace to an existing home?

Usually, yes. Direct vent fireplaces can be installed in most rooms with an exterior wall. We'll assess your space and explain your options.

How much heat does a gas fireplace produce?

Varies by model, but many produce 20,000-40,000 BTUs—enough to heat a large room or supplement your main heating system significantly.

How often should I service my gas fireplace?

Once a year is recommended. We'll clean the glass, check the pilot and ignition system, inspect venting, and make sure everything is safe.

My pilot light keeps going out. Is that dangerous?

Modern gas fireplaces have safety systems that shut off gas flow if the pilot fails. Not dangerous, but annoying—and usually an easy fix.
