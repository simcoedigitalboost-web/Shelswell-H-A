Why Consider a Heat Pump?

Heat pumps are one of the most efficient ways to heat and cool your home. Here's why homeowners are making the switch:

  • One system for both heating and cooling
  • Up to 3x more efficient than traditional heating
  • Lower monthly energy bills
  • Reduced carbon footprint
  • Government rebates often available
  • Quiet operation

Types of Heat Pumps

We install and service multiple types of heat pump systems to fit your home and budget:

  • Air-source heat pumps (most common)
  • Ductless mini-splits for homes without ductwork
  • Hybrid systems paired with gas furnace backup
  • Cold-climate heat pumps rated for Ontario winters

Not sure which is right for you? We'll assess your home and give you honest recommendations.
