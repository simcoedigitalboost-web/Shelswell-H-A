Water Heaters: Tank & Tankless

Nothing ruins a morning like a cold shower. Whether your water heater is on its last legs or you're ready to upgrade to tankless, we'll get your hot water flowing—usually the same day.

Tank water heaters

The classic choice. Tank water heaters are reliable, affordable, and we can usually get you back in hot water the same day your old one fails.

We install quality brands like Rheem that are built to last. Proper installation matters—the right sizing, proper venting, and code-compliant gas connections make all the difference in lifespan and efficiency.

  • 40, 50, and 60 gallon options
  • Gas and electric models
  • Same-day installation available
  • Proper disposal of old unit

Tankless water heaters

Endless hot water, lower energy bills, and a unit that takes up almost no space. Tankless water heaters heat water on demand—no tank sitting there keeping 50 gallons hot whether you need it or not.

We install Navien and other quality tankless units. They cost more upfront, but many homeowners find the energy savings and endless hot water worth it.

  • Never run out of hot water
  • 20+ year lifespan (vs. 10-12 for tanks)
  • Wall-mounted, saves floor space
  • Lower monthly energy costs

Common Questions

Tank or tankless—which is right for me?

Depends on your household size, hot water usage, and budget. Tankless costs more upfront but saves on energy. We'll help you weigh the options honestly.

How long does water heater installation take?

Tank replacement is typically 2-4 hours. Tankless installations take longer, especially if gas line or venting upgrades are needed—usually a full day.

My water heater is leaking. Is it an emergency?

A small drip from the relief valve might not be urgent. A puddle forming underneath usually means the tank is failing—call us and we'll get there as quickly as possible.

How long do water heaters last?

Tank water heaters typically last 10-12 years. Tankless units can go 20+ years with proper maintenance. If yours is approaching that age, it's smart to plan ahead.
