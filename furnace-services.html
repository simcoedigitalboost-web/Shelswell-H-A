Signs Your Furnace Needs Attention

Don't wait for a complete breakdown. Watch for these warning signs that indicate your furnace may need repair or replacement:

  • Unusual noises like banging, squealing, or rattling
  • Uneven heating or cold spots in your home
  • Increased energy bills without explanation
  • Frequent cycling on and off
  • Yellow pilot light (should be blue)
  • Furnace is 15+ years old

If you notice any of these signs, give us a call. We'll diagnose the issue and give you honest advice—even if that means a simple repair rather than a replacement.
